Cricket Pitch Detection using OpenCV-Python

This project focuses on detecting the Cricket pitch and Stumpline In the next version it going to detect the cricket ball using computer vision techniques to determine where the ball lands on the pitch during delivery. Leveraging high-frame-rate video footage and OpenCV, the system tracks the ball trajectory frame-by-frame and identifies the point of impact on the pitch surface. The project also incorporates deep learning with YOLOv8 for ball detection and object tracking algorithms (e.g., SORT or Deep SORT) to maintain consistency across frames.
